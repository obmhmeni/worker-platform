Data Policy

WorkerHire ensures that all personal and professional data submitted by users is securely stored in protected databases. Our system uses modern authentication methods and encrypted connections to protect information from unauthorized access. Workers and recruiters can safely interact within the platform without risk of privacy violations. Recruiters are only able to view relevant professional information required for hiring decisions, while workers retain full control of their personal profile details. This ensures transparency and safety for both parties involved. WorkerHire aims to create a trusted environment where skilled professionals can connect with companies efficiently while maintaining strict privacy standards.
